HooksyNest Cookie Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2023

At HooksyNest, we believe in transparency and want you to understand how we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the website owners.

Cookies perform various functions, such as remembering your preferences, improving your browsing experience, and helping us understand how our website is being used. They cannot be used to identify you personally.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use several types of cookies on our website:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be switched off. They include cookies that enable you to log in to secure areas of our website or use a shopping cart.
  • Performance Cookies: These allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site.
  • Functional Cookies: These enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences.
  • Targeting Cookies: These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests.
  • Third-Party Cookies: We use some third-party cookies for analytics and social media integration.

Detailed Cookie Information

Below is a detailed list of the cookies we use on HooksyNest:

Essential Cookies

  • session_cookie: Maintains your session state across page requests (duration: session)
  • auth_token: Keeps you logged in when moving between pages (duration: 30 days)
  • cart_id: Remembers items in your shopping cart (duration: 7 days)

Performance Cookies

  • _ga: Google Analytics cookie used to distinguish users (duration: 2 years)
  • _gid: Google Analytics cookie used to distinguish users (duration: 24 hours)
  • _gat: Google Analytics cookie used to throttle request rate (duration: 1 minute)

Functional Cookies

  • language_pref: Remembers your language preference (duration: 1 year)
  • theme_pref: Stores your selected color theme (duration: 1 year)
  • recent_patterns: Remembers recently viewed patterns (duration: 30 days)

Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several important reasons:

Essential Website Operations

Certain cookies are essential for our website to function properly. Without these cookies, features like shopping carts, secure login areas, and account management would not work correctly. These cookies don't gather information about you that could be used for marketing or remembering where you've been on the internet.

Improving Your Experience

We use cookies to remember your preferences and settings, such as language preference, font size, and color scheme. This means you don't have to keep re-entering information whenever you come back to our site or browse from one page to another.

Understanding Our Audience

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by providing information about the areas visited, the time spent on the website, and any issues encountered, such as error messages. This helps us improve the performance of our website.

Marketing and Personalization

We use cookies to deliver content that is more relevant to you and your interests. They may be used to deliver targeted advertising or to limit the number of times you see an advertisement. These cookies may remember that you have visited our website and share this information with other organizations such as advertisers.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies on our website:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it. You can also delete cookies that have already been set and prevent your browser from accepting new cookies.

To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit www.aboutcookies.org or www.allaboutcookies.org.

Opt-Out Tools

For analytics cookies, you can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing Google's opt-out browser add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

For advertising cookies, you can visit the Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out page at https://optout.aboutads.info.

Our Cookie Consent Tool

When you first visit our website, you'll see our cookie consent banner that allows you to accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customize your preferences. You can change these settings at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of our website.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our data use practices. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.

If you have any questions about our use of cookies or this Cookie Policy, please contact us using the information provided in the contact section below.

Cookie-Related Contact Information

1. Cookie Consent Management

If you need assistance managing your cookie preferences on our website, our support team can guide you through the process. 3. Questions About Specific Cookies

For detailed information about a specific cookie we use that isn't covered in our Cookie Policy, you can contact our data protection officer at dpo@hooksynest.com. Please specify the cookie name (visible in your browser's developer tools) and what information you're seeking. We aim to respond to these inquiries within 5 business days with comprehensive details about the cookie's purpose, duration, and data collected.

4. Third-Party Cookie Inquiries

For questions about third-party cookies used on our site (such as those from analytics or advertising partners), we can provide information about these relationships and how to opt-out directly with those providers. While we vet all third-party services for compliance with privacy laws, you may need to contact them directly for certain requests. Our support team can direct you to the appropriate contacts at support@hooksynest.com.

5. Cookie Policy Clarifications

If any part of our Cookie Policy is unclear or you need further explanation about our practices, our customer service team is happy to help. We can provide simplified explanations, examples, or additional context to help you understand how and why we use cookies. Reach us at hello@hooksynest.com or call our toll-free number for immediate assistance during business hours.

6. Legal Compliance Questions

For questions about our compliance with cookie-related regulations (such as GDPR, CCPA, or ePrivacy Directive), please contact our legal team at legal@hooksynest.com. 7. Accessibility Concerns

If you have difficulty accessing or using our cookie consent tools due to accessibility needs, please contact our accessibility team at accessibility@hooksynest.com. We're committed to making all aspects of our website, including cookie management, accessible to all users and can provide alternative methods for setting your preferences if needed.

8. Business Partner Cookie Inquiries

Vendors, advertisers, and other business partners with questions about cookie implementation or data sharing should contact our partnerships team at partnerships@hooksynest.com. 9. Cookie Security Concerns

If you have security concerns related to our use of cookies (such as suspected malicious activity or vulnerabilities), please immediately contact our security team at security@hooksynest.com. For questions about our cookie practices regarding children's data (we do not knowingly collect data from children under 16 without parental consent), contact our child privacy officer at privacy@hooksynest.com.
