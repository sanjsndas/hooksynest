Frequently Asked Questions

What are your business hours?

Our physical store is open Monday through Friday from 9am to 5pm PST, and Saturdays from 10am to 3pm PST. Our online shop is always open! Customer support responds to emails within 24 hours during weekdays.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also log in to your account on our website to view order status and tracking details. If you're having trouble tracking your package, contact our support team with your order number.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. During checkout, you'll see the available shipping options and costs for your location. Please note that international orders may be subject to customs fees imposed by your country.

Can I return or exchange yarn?

We accept returns of unused yarn in its original condition within 30 days of purchase. The customer is responsible for return shipping costs. For exchanges, please contact us first to ensure availability of the desired item. Unfortunately, we cannot accept returns on digital patterns or opened kits.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes! During checkout, you can select our gift wrapping option for $5. Your gift will be beautifully wrapped in our signature HooksyNest packaging with a handwritten note (if you'd like to include a message). We also offer gift cards in various denominations that can be emailed or mailed to the recipient.

How do I join your crochet-alongs (CALs)?

We host regular crochet-alongs in our online community. To join, simply purchase the pattern (or use a free one if specified), then participate in our discussion forums where members share progress, ask questions, and cheer each other on. Some CALs have special prizes for participants!
